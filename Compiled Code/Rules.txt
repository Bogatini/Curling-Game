Click, drag back and release the curling stones to throw them
Once a stone is thrown you can scratch up the ice to increase friction and curve stones by left clicking on the ice with a short cooldown

There are two teams, blue and red whos scores are displayed at the end of the match
The closer to the centre of the target a stone is, the more points the team receives at the end of the match
Scores are calculated at the very end of the match so it is onlt the stones final position that affects their score
